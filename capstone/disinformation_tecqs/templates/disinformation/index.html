About
The game is a choose your own adventure type of game which will tackles about political disinformation present in the Philippines. The web application is based on research entitled "Architects of Networked Disinformation: Behind the Scenes of Troll Accounts and Fake News Production in the Philippines".

The web project aims to raise awareness and produce media literate users to prevent the spread of political disinformation; and for them to not become a future victim. The game has a total of three stages, each has a political disinformation technique that will be applied, for them to know how this technique posed a threat in the community.
Creator
I am Eunice Patricia M. Villanueva; a 3rd year iskolar in the Polytechnic University of the Philippines; Sta. Mesa, Manila. I aim to educate my peers and other people belonging to the same community as I am about disinformation, most especially in politics; to produce wise voters for the next election and prevent the hindrance to elect the right leaders of the country towards the great future of the Philippines.
